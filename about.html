Why we decided to create this tour guide website in Lviv, Ukraine?

  • Tourism has the potential to stimulate economic recovery in areas affected by the war. Creating a tour guide website that showcases Lviv's attractions, cultural heritage, and unique experiences can attract tourists despite the challenging circumstances. The influx of visitors can help revitalize local businesses and contribute to the city's economic recovery.
  • Peacebuilding Efforts: Tourism can contribute to peacebuilding efforts by promoting people-to-people connections and cultural exchange. It can help break down barriers and stereotypes, encouraging mutual understanding and dialogue between visitors and locals.
  • A well-crafted tour guide website can provide accurate and up-to-date information about the situation in Lviv, reassuring potential tourists about the safety measures in place and the peaceful atmosphere in the city.
